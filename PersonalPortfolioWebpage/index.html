My Latest Projects

SnapShare - iOS

Big Red Shuttle is a group at Cornell that provides free shuttle bus service around Cornell's campus late nights and on weekends. CUAppDev partnered with Big Red Shuttle to create BRShuttle, a simple way to check the bus route and plan your trip home. The app was designed with simplicity and user safety in mind, we have easy access to the schedule as well as emergency services in case someone runs into an issue when trying to get home late at night. As a core team developer of CUAppDev, I helped create BRShuttle alongside 35 talented designers and developers. Our open-source repo can be found on Github. You can find the current release on the App Store.
