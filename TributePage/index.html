Joe Rogan

Stand up comic, mix martial arts fanatic, psychedelic adventurer, and host of the JRE podcast

Here's a time line of Joe Rogan's life:

  • 1976 - Born in New Jersey on and raised in Boston, Massachusetts
  • 1988 - Began performing stand-up around Boston
  • 1995 - Rogan's first big break came, when he was cast as handyman Joe Girelli on the NBC sitcom . The show ran until 1999
  • 2000 - Releases "I'm Gonna be Dead Someday" comedy special
  • 2001 - Became the host of the hugely successful reality show Fear Factor, which ran for six seasons on NBC
  • 2001 - Releases "Live From the Belly of the Beast" comedy special
  • 2002 - Became a color commentator for the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) Pay Per View bouts. Rogan himself has been an accomplished martial artists since age 13; he holds a black belt in Tae Kwon Do and a brown belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
  • 2006 - Releases "Joe Rogan: Live" comedy special
  • 2007 - Releases "Shiny Happy Jihad" comedy special
  • 2009 - Began hosting his own comedy podcast, The Joe Rogan Experience. The show would eventually gain more than 10 million listeners and be syndicated on SIRIUS XM satellite radio
  • 2010 - Releases "Flying Monkeys from Space" comedy special
  • 2012 - Releases "Live from the Tabernacle" comedy special
  • 2013 - Became the host of his own original series on SyFy, Joe Rogan Questions Everything, which ran for 13 episodes
  • 2014 - Releases "Rocky Mountain High" comedy special
  • 2016 - Releases "Triggered" comedy special
  • 2018 - Releases "Strange Times" comedy special
